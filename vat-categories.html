South African VAT Categories

Standard Rated (15%)

  • Most goods and services
  • Consulting services
  • Retail sales
  • Professional services
  • Digital goods and software
  • Office utilities and consumables

Zero Rated (0%)

  • Brown bread
  • Maize meal
  • Milk
  • Fruit and vegetables (certain items)
  • Fuel levy goods
  • Municipal rates
  • International transport of goods/passengers

Exempt Supplies

  • Residential rentals
  • Certain education services
  • Some public transport
  • Financial services
  • Sale of land and improvements outside of SA
  • Supply of goods by Non-Resident suppliers
